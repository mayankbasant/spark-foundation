The COVID-19 pandemic has shown us how challenging it is for children in rural and semi-urban areas to access quality education, whether online or offline. The effect of the lockdown has brought education to a standstill for millions of rural children. Since 2011, eVidyaloka has been using technology to empower rural children by connecting passionate volunteers across the globe, online. While students in cities attend online classes from the comfort of their homes, most students across Rural India have limited access to Internet-enabled devices and teachers equipped to teach online.
